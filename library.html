VRC Digital & Physical Library

A comprehensive catalogue of Ramayana-related titles currently available in the VRC Library is accessible for public reference. As part of its ambitious first-year goal, the Centre aims to collect a minimum of 1,00,000 Ramayana-related literary works.

VRC has initiated collaborations with prominent libraries across Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Thanjavur, Kolkata, Varanasi, and Kanchi Kamakoti Peetham. In parallel, an extensive e-book collection of ancient and digitized Ramayana texts is being compiled to ensure preservation and global accessibility.

  • Multi-State Library Collaborations

Library Guidelines

General Rules & Conduct

Members must handle all library materials with care and respect. Silence must be maintained in the library premises at all times.

  • Personal belongings (except notebooks) are not allowed inside the library reading area.
  • Mobile phones must be switched off or kept in silent mode within the library.
  • Eating, sleeping, and talking loudly are strictly prohibited inside the library.
  • Strict decorum and discipline must be maintained at all times.

Issue, Return & Penalties

  • If a book is damaged or lost, double the original cost will be charged. For rare books, triple the cost will be charged.
  • Fine of ₹2 per day (including weekends & holidays) for the first 7 days of delay.
  • After 7 days, ₹10 per day for the next 7 days; thereafter membership may be cancelled.
  • The book will be reissued only once.

Usage & Access Regulations

  • Identity card must be produced and entry recorded in the register.
  • Library materials are strictly for reading/reference purposes only.
  • No material may be taken outside without permission from the library in-charge.
  • Non-members may use library materials only with Director’s permission.
  • The librarian may recall any book at any time if required.

Users are advised not to leave valuables in deposited bags. The Library will not be responsible for any loss.
