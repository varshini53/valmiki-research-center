Valmiki Research Centre

The Valmiki Research Centre (VRC) regards the Ramayana, composed by the great sage Valmiki, as an invaluable treasure of humanity — one of the world’s most ancient and timeless epics. Beyond religious origins, it offers universal insights into ethics, leadership, duty, and spiritual wisdom.
