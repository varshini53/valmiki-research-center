Research Programs in Ramayana Studies

Fundamental and interdisciplinary research programs

Program Overview

The Research Programs in Ramayana Studies aim to promote original and interdisciplinary research by integrating traditional Indian knowledge systems with contemporary academic disciplines. The programs follow the National Education Policy (NEP) interdisciplinary framework.

Research Focus Areas

  • Application of Ramayana to modern societal challenges
  • Vedanta and Dharma Shastra
  • Sociology, Public Administration & Management
  • Geography, Psychology & Philosophy
  • Fine Arts, Medicinal, Historical & Archaeological studies
  • Artistic and cultural exploration

Research Program Information

Eligibility: Postgraduates, faculty & researchers

Approach: Interdisciplinary (NEP aligned)

Objective: Blend traditional wisdom with modern issues
