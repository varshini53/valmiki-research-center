About Valmiki Research Centre

RAMANARAYANAM is a spiritual theme park with an aesthetic gateway for those seeking solace in a profound spiritual experience. The unique design and elevation of the PRANGANAM leave tourists and pilgrims spellbound with an experience that is rich, cherishing, and invigorating.When noble thoughts transform into great intentions, and such intentions evolve into a driving passion, marvels like RAMANARAYANAM are created. Located on Korukonda Road, Vizianagaram — just 50 km from Vizag Airport, Andhra Pradesh — this theme park has set new standards, attracting visitors from across the globe.“JAI SRI RAM”, this magnum opus stands as a landmark in the historic city of Vizianagaram — the city of learning and music.

Valmiki Research Centre

The Valmiki Research Centre (VRC) regards the Ramayana, composed by the great sage Valmiki, as an invaluable treasure of humanity — one of the world’s most ancient and timeless epics. Beyond religious origins, it offers universal insights into ethics, leadership, duty, and spiritual wisdom.
