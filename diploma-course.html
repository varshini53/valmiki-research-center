Diploma Program in Ramayana Studies

Structured and in-depth academic exploration of the Ramayana

Program Overview

The Diploma Program in Ramayana Studies is a comprehensive academic course designed for scholars, students, and spiritual seekers who wish to explore the Ramayana in depth. The program integrates philosophy, literature, ethics, and cultural studies rooted in the epic composed by Sage Valmiki.

Curriculum Highlights

  • Origins and historical context of the Ramayana
  • Character studies and ethical teachings
  • Philosophical interpretations
  • Cultural and societal impact
  • Comparative studies and modern relevance

Course Information

Duration: 1 Year

Mode: Offline / Hybrid

Eligibility: Open to all graduates

Certification: Diploma Certificate
