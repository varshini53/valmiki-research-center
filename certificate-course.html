Certificate Course in Ramayana Studies

Foundational and value-based study of the Ramayana

Program Overview

The Certificate Course in Ramayana Studies is a short-term foundational program designed for students, temple Archakas, employees, and individuals interested in spiritual and cultural learning. This course can also be pursued as an add-on course along with regular academic studies.

Course Focus

  • Kanda-wise study of the Ramayana
  • Moral values from main characters
  • Philosophical teachings (Vedanta, Sankhya, Bhakti)
  • Impact of Ramayana on society
  • Contemporary relevance

Course Information

Duration: 6 Months

Eligibility: 10th / Intermediate / Equivalent

Age Limit: No age limit

Medium: Telugu (support in English, Hindi & Sanskrit)

Papers: 5 Core Subjects

Mode: Blended Learning (Online + Offline)

Special Feature: A.P. Endowment Dept exam aligned
